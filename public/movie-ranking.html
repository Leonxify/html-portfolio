The Best Anime According to Alfred

My top 3 anime of all-time.

Classroom of the Elite

Classroom of the Elite is a Japanese light novel and anime series centered on a meritocratic high school where students compete to reach the top class, following the enigmatic Kiyotaka Ayanokōji.

Ninja Kamui

Ninja Kamui is an anime series that follows Joe Logan, a former ninja who seeks revenge after his family is brutally attacked by his old clan.

Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a popular anime series that follows high school student Yuji Itadori as he battles curses and navigates the complexities of being a Jujutsu Sorcerer after becoming the host of a powerful curse named Ryomen Sukuna.
